Concept Art

I've always had a love for drawing. Simply, because it let's you create and visualize the things in your head. With my love for gaming I started to look at how games and every process of it was made. There is a lot involved level-design, audio and sound, 3D moddeling and of course concept art. I fell in love with it the first time I saw it and I still love it. For me it's a way to visualize a piece of the small universe of ideas that is my mind.

Photography

I started with photography at an early age. I think I should say and interest in photograpy, because I really started doing my own when I got a phone with a camera. From there on out I always made sure I had the phone with the best camera options, so I could make pictures on the fly wherever I was and then upload them to my pc. Eventually this wasn't enough anymore, because I wanted to make pictures at night and in situations where there is very little light. So I bought my first DSLR camera and a tripod (which I both still have to this day) and from there I started making lots of pictures of things that interest me, as well as material I could use as inspiration for my concept art.

Food

Food is something everybody needs, but making food and making it look good are two different things altogether. Once again this started out as liked the food that I tasted in different places and then beingt curious as to how they were made because I wanted to reproduce them. This grew out to be a big hobby of mine and you will often see me reading or watching foodblogs or recipes to see how I can improve myself in making delicious and beautiful things.
